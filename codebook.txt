X - Index of the vehicle in the dataset

price - The price of the vehicle on the auction

brand - The brand of the vehicle on the auction (ex: a Toyota car)

model - The model of the brand of the vehicle on the auction (ex: Toyota Prius)

year - The registration year for the vehicle

color - The color of the vehicle

mileage - How many miles the vehicle has traveled

vin - The iD for a vehicle; 17 character string combination of letters and numbers

title_status - Binary classification variable; either clean (alternative fuels from gas)
or salvage (vehicle has suffered past damage and thus has little value)

lot - The iD number for a vehicle that belongs to a given quantity; for instance there would be
a lot number for a specific batch of Priuses

state - The US state where the car is available to be purchased

country - The country where the car is available to be purchased

condition - Time left on the auction to bid for the vehicle
